Executive Summary¶

This project analyzes two research questions. The datasets used is from grandslam winner information as well as grand slam tournament information. The focus is on the the nationalities, prize money, dominant hand of the winners, and tournament surface. \ One finding is that the French Open is the highest paying grand slam tournament and there is a significant correlation with prize money and years.\ One finding is that Spainiards are the most successful on clay, Americans are the most successful on Outdoor Grass and DecoTurn Outdoors followed closely by Australians on both surfaces, and Australians are the most successful on Plexicushion Prestige.\ One finding is that right hand dominant players are favored in every surface, but they are most dominant on Outdoor Grass and Plexicushion Prestige.

Research Questions¶

  1. Which grand slams offer more prize money over the years?
  2. Which nationalities are the most successful winning on which surfaces?
  3. Are left or right hand dominant players most successful on which surfaces?

Data Manipulation¶

I added median values for the missing values for WinnerRank and RunnerUpRank because I wanted to add values that did not change the mean of the data too much. In this case, median is a better measure than mean because rankings are whole numbers and the means would be floats. There were missing values for prize money but I did not want to change the data and I will mention that the prize money data is from 1970-2023.
